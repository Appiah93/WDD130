MONROVIA, Liberia

Liberia, located on the west coast of Africa, is a country rich in history and cultural diversity. Established by freed American and Caribbean slaves in 1822, it declared its independence in 1847, making it Africa's oldest republic. The country is known for its lush rainforests, scenic coastline, and significant natural resources such as rubber and iron ore. Despite a tumultuous past marked by civil wars in the late 20th and early 21st centuries, Liberia has been striving towards stability and economic recovery. The capital city, Monrovia, serves as the political, cultural, and economic hub, reflecting both the challenges and the resilience of its people.
